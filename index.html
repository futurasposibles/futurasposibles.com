Futuras Posibles is a journey—or rather, multiple journeys at once—that embrace many cracks: my own and those of those who accompany me in this process of migration. With it, I aim to reflect on my ongoing pursuit of identity in unfamiliar surroundings by reappropriating Google’s online tools and programs from a design perspective, serving as a metaphor for my own migratory process and adaptation to new territories. This project is at once an exploration, a conversation, and an invitation—to venture into the unknown while sharing the experiences of others.
               Thank you for taking the time to embark on this journey. I hope you enjoy navigating it as much as I enjoyed creating it.
